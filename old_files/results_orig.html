Resonances and Scattering Phase Shifts

Isospin-1 P-wave ππ elastic scattering phase shift (relevant for the ρ resonance) and a Breit-Wigner parameterisation, from a dynamical lattice QCD calculation on several volumes with mπ = 391 MeV. Energy region plotted is from ππ threshold to KK threshold. [Phys. Rev. D 87, 034505 (2013)]



Elastic phase shifts for isospin-2 ππ scattering in l = 0 (δ0) and 2 (δ2) as a function of scattering momentum in the center-of-momentum frame, from a dynamical lattice QCD calculation on several volumes with mπ ≈ 396 MeV. Coloured points correspond to an analysis treating each energy level independently and curves indicate a global analysis of all energy levels using a scattering length or effective range parameterisation. (More details are given in the paper.) [Phys. Rev. D 86, 034031 (2012)]


Meson Spectroscopy - Light Isoscalars

Isoscalar meson spectrum labelled by JPC in a dynamical lattice QCD calculation (mπ ≈ 396 MeV). The box height denotes the statistical uncertainty, the light-strange content of each state is given by the fraction of black-green and the mixing angle is also shown. Grey boxes indicate the positions of isovector meson states extracted on the same lattice, pink boxes indicate the position of quenched glueballs from a previous calculation. This is the first lattice calculation of light isoscalar exotics; these appear at a mass scale comparable to their isovector cousins. [Phys. Rev. D 83, 111502 (2011)]


Meson Spectroscopy - Light Isovectors

Masses of the lowest-lying isovector mesons with exotic quantum numbers (JPC) in a dynamical lattice QCD calculation on two volumes and at four quark masses. For comparison, a compendium of results from other groups for the 1-+ exotic, in both quenched and full QCD, is also plotted. [Phys. Rev. D 82, 034508 (2010)]



The extracted isovector meson spectrum, classified by continuum JPC, on dynamical lattices with three degenerate quarks (mπ = MK = Mη ≈ 700 MeV), in units of MΩ. Boxes represent the extracted mass and one sigma statistical uncertainties. This is the first time a spin-four meson has been extracted in Lattice QCD; exotic mesons are also highlighted. [Phys. Rev. Lett. 103 262001 (2009) & Phys. Rev. D 82, 034508 (2010)]


Meson Spectroscopy - Charmonium

Charmonium spectrum labelled by JPC in a dynamical lattice QCD calculation (mπ ≈ 396 MeV). States with exotic JPC are shown in the right panel. Boxes represent the extracted mass and one sigma statistical uncertainties; black lines are experimental values from the PDG. We show the calculated (experimental) masses with the calculated (experimental) ηc mass subtracted. The dashed lines indicate the lowest non-interacting D D and Ds Ds levels using masses measured on the lattice (fine green dashing) and using the experimental masses (coarse grey dashing). [JHEP 1207 (2012) 126]


Baryon Spectroscopy

The spectrum of excited isospin-1/2 baryon (nucleon) resonances in two lattice irreps (Hu and G2u) on lattices with mπ = 392, 438, 521 MeV; the heights of the colored boxes give the 1σ statistical uncertainty on either side of the extracted energies. Also shown are two, three and four-star experimental resonances, labelled by JP, which appear in the irrep; the inner boxes (aqua) show the uncertainty on the resonance energies and the outer boxes (white) give the full decay widths. [Phys. Rev. D 82, 014507 (2010)]


Meson Radiative Transitions

Electric dipole transition form-factors for the first and second excited vector state to the ground state scalar in charmonium. Experimental results for real photons from PDG and CLEO-c. [Phys. Rev. D 79, 094504 (2009)]
